# charliebox

**Encrypted, Git-federated personal communication platform.**

Your space. Your people. Your things. No spam. No phishing. No server.

End-to-end encrypted (age) | Federated over Git (GitHub, GitLab, Gitea, self-hosted) | Spam-proof by architecture (not by filters) | Email, calendar, notes, voice, files — all in one | Runs on your hardware (Git host is dumb storage) | Unlimited identities / avatars | Auto-imports your legacy email

**Status:** Pre-release / Active development
